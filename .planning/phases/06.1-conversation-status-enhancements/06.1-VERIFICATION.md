**Expected:** Counter climbs: 1s, 2s, 3s, 4s, 5s... without capping at 3s (the chunk silence threshold). When speech is detected, the counter resets immediately to "Silence: 0s".
**Why human:** The cross-chunk silence accumulation logic (_silence_offset_sec) is structurally verified but the counter-climbing-past-3s behavior and the reset-on-voice responsiveness require live audio hardware and a running GTK event loop.

### 2. Transcript Card Display

**Test:** Speak a sentence, wait for silence boundary (3s default), observe the card frame in the status window.
**Expected:** After the chunk transcribes, transcribed words appear inside the card frame with GNOME card styling. After a second chunk, the card updates to the new text, replacing the previous.
**Why human:** Requires live Whisper API call and GTK rendering to confirm the card frame renders correctly and text updates per-chunk.
